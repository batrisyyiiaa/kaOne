FAQ

Common Questions

Is my financial data secure? +
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256. We never share your data with third parties. Your API keys and transactions are stored only on your device's local session.
How accurate is the tax prediction? +
Our GEMINI-2.5-flash powered tax forecaster achieves ~98% accuracy based on your income history. It accounts for LHDN rates, PCB deductions, and business expense categories relevant to Malaysian tax law.
Do I need to link my bank account? +
No. You can simply upload a CSV export from your bank app, take a screenshot of your transactions, or enter data manually. We don't require direct bank access.
Is this really free? +
Yes! The Freemium plan gives you access to core features. Premium unlocks unlimited AI chat, advanced forecasting, and priority support. We believe every Malaysian small business owner deserves smart financial tools.
